Our features

These are only part of the capabilities we offer to our clients as part of the product we developed.

No Subscription Fees

Pay once for the device and installation. No monthly plans, no renewals, no hidden fees - now or ever.

Advanced Security Architecture

Engineered for uncompromising protection with Secure Boot (RSA-3072), encrypted flash storage (AES-128/256-XTS), a Trusted Execution Environment, HMAC encryption, and anti replay attack.

No Internet Exposure

Your data never leaves your premises. User databases stay on-device, fully encrypted and isolated from the internet.

Guest Access Pass

Let guests in without the interruptions. Share a temporary access pass from the app so visitors can open the gate on their own, no more calls asking you to open the gate.

Any-Way Access

Open your gate using the mobile app or traditional RF remotes, whichever suits you best.

Simple User Management

Easily manage users and permissions through the mobile app or by importing a CSV file.
